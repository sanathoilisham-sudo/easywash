About Us

EasyWash is a professional laundry and garment care brand operated by LG Enterprises, based in Khongman Zone III, Imphal East, Manipur. We provide high-quality laundry services including regular wash, dry cleaning, blanket wash, bulk wash, ironing, and doorstep pickup and delivery.

Our mission is to make laundry hassle-free for families and businesses in Imphal. With careful handling, eco-friendly detergents, and timely service, we ensure your garments are returned fresh, clean, and well-cared for.

We also offer additional services including driving licence assistance, vehicle insurance, and other public services through LG Enterprises to serve our community better.
